About Me

With a BSc in Graphic Design and MSc in Illustration from Iran's premier art university, I bring a decade of professional experience to every project. My work spans corporate identity, digital design, and artistic illustration, combining technical precision with creative vision.

I'm proficient in the entire Adobe Creative Suite, specializing in Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, and Lightroom. My expertise extends to digital painting and professional photography, allowing me to offer comprehensive visual solutions.

Beyond commercial work, I maintain an active artistic practice, sharing illustrations and paintings through my Instagram platform, where I believe in making art accessible to all.
